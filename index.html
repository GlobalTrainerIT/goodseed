Questions & answers

What is GoodSeed?

GoodSeed is a faith-based chore and rewards app for families. Parents create tasks, kids earn Seeds for completing them, and Seeds are redeemed for real rewards the family sets up — extra screen time, choosing dinner, a special outing. It layers in streaks, levels, badges, family goals, and a daily Bible verse.

Is it free?

Yes — the Free plan includes 1 parent, up to 2 children, and the full chore-and-rewards loop on one device, forever. GoodSeed Plus ($4.99/month) adds unlimited children, co-parents, and live sync across every family member's device.

Can my kids use their own devices?

With Plus, yes. Kids join with your 6-character family code on their tablet or phone and everything syncs in real time. A parent PIN keeps admin actions locked on shared devices.

Do I need to install anything?

No download needed — it runs in your browser. On a phone you can "Add to Home Screen" and it installs like a native app, offline included.

What makes it faith-based?

GoodSeed is built around encouragement and Christian family values: a rotating daily Bible verse, kindness tasks, family shout-outs, and cooperative goals the whole family works toward together.

Can coaches or teachers use GoodSeed?

Yes! GoodSeed for Teams & Classrooms lets coaches, teachers, daycares, and youth leaders build a roster (kids join by first name — no accounts), award or deduct points with a tap during practice or class, and run a live leaderboard. It's $12.99/mo or $119/yr with a 30-day free trial, and organization pricing is available. Learn more.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plus is billed monthly through Stripe — cancel anytime in Settings → Manage billing and keep access through the end of your paid period.
