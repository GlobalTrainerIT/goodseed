🤝 Our Parent Promise

Your family's trust is the whole product.

GoodSeed exists to help you raise kids with good character. That only works if you can trust us completely — so here is our promise, in plain English, no legal degree required.

🚫

We will never sell your family's data.

Not to advertisers, not to "partners," not to anyone. Your family's information is not our product — the app is.

📵

No ads. No tracking. Especially not for kids.

There are zero advertisements in GoodSeed and zero analytics trackers following your family around. We don't build profiles of anyone — least of all children.

👨‍👩‍👧

A child can never sign up alone.

Every child profile is created by a parent, and a child's device can only join with the code you choose to share. You are the gatekeeper — always.

🤏

We collect the bare minimum.

A child's profile is just a first name, an optional age, and an avatar. No child emails, no passwords, no location — because the safest data is the data we never ask for.

🗑️

You can delete everything, anytime.

One button in Settings permanently removes your family's data from your devices and our database. No phone calls, no "retention offers," no waiting period.

🔒

Your family's data is locked to your family.

Everything is encrypted, and our database enforces family-by-family walls — no other family (and no stranger) can ever read yours.

“Let your ‘Yes’ be yes, and your ‘No,’ no.” MATTHEW 5:37

The details live in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Questions? Email dirtyhandsdispatch@gmail.com — a human reads every message.
